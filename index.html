    Introducing an extensible editot

    BlogMe features an exciting intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos, and Markdown. Extensibility with plugins and themes provide easy way to add functionality or change the looks of a blog

    Robust content management

    Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Increase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format, or flow. With this functionality, you're in full control.

    State of the Art Infrastructure

    With reliability and speed in mind, worldwide data centers provide the backbone for ultra-fast connectivty. Tgis ensures site will load instantly, no matter where your readers are, keeping your site competitive.

    Free, open, simple

    BlogMe is a free and open source applicaton backed by a large community of helpful developers. It supports features such as code syntax highlighting, RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The architecture is clean and is relively easy to learn.

    Powerful tooling

    Batteries included. We built a simple and straightforward CLI tool that makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of producing even the most complicated sites.
